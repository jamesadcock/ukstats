# About UK Stats

UK Stats is an independent, non-partisan website that presents official UK public statistics in a clear and accessible format.

## Why we built it

Official statistics are often buried inside government PDFs, surrounded by caveats, or presented without useful context. Our goal is to surface the numbers that matter — and only those numbers — without spin, commentary, or political framing.

## What we publish

Every statistic on UK Stats comes from a named official source — primarily the **Office for National Statistics (ONS)**, but also NHS England, the Department for Education, HM Land Registry, and other accredited UK statistical bodies.

We do not commission original research. We do not adjust or re-weight data. We present official figures as published, with full source attribution.

## Editorial principles

1. **Accuracy over speed.** We update figures when we can verify the source has published new data.
2. **No spin.** We do not frame statistics as "good" or "bad". We present the number, its unit, and its context.
3. **Full transparency.** Every stat page links to the primary source. Methodology notes explain how the figure is measured.
4. **Accessibility.** All charts have data table equivalents. The site meets WCAG 2.1 AA.

## Who we are

UK Stats is run by a small team of developers and data enthusiasts. We are not affiliated with the UK government, ONS, or any political organisation.
